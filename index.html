Some frequently asked questions

You can subscribe to ClimInvest in three simple ways: 1) Call 980 (free) and speak with our advisors, 2) Send SMS "MON ASSURANCE AGRICOLE" to 980, or 3) Download our mobile app. All methods provide the same comprehensive agricultural insurance coverage.

Your premiums create value beyond insurance! We provide you with agricultural credit scoring, soil quality analysis, yield predictions, and market price insights. You also get access to financing opportunities and personalized farming advice to optimize your production and sales.

No problem! Call 980 (free) to speak with our multilingual advisors, or send SMS "MON ASSURANCE AGRICOLE" to 980. You can also ask a neighbor with a smartphone to help you register through our community assistance program.

ClimInvest provides automatic payouts within 24-72 hours when climate events are detected by our OpenEPI integration. This is 45x faster than traditional insurance which takes 3-6 months for compensation.

We use advanced satellite technology and OpenEPI data to monitor crop health in real-time. Our system cross-validates with local weather stations and provides transparent thresholds. For major claims (>25,000 FCFA), we also conduct on-site expert visits to ensure accuracy.

Currently, ClimInvest covers climate-related risks: drought (>21 days without rain), floods detected by satellite, and storms with winds >118 km/h. Plant diseases and insect attacks are not covered yet, but we're planning to extend coverage in 2026. We provide recommendations for pest prevention and partner with local phytosanitary services.

Your data is fully encrypted and protected. We don't share personal information with commercial third parties. Only anonymized statistics are shared with government for agricultural policies and researchers for climate studies. You have full control over your data and can access, modify, or delete it anytime.

We provide real-time payment tracking with SMS notifications at each step. If payment is delayed beyond 48h, we automatically escalate and apply 5% compensation per day of delay. You can track your payment status anytime by calling 980 or through our app.

We've simplified our alerts! Instead of technical terms, you'll receive clear messages like "🌵 Your crops are thirsty - water if possible" or "🌊 High flood risk - protect your harvest". We also provide voice messages in local languages (Fon, Yoruba, Bambara) with practical action advice.

Yes! Our Analytics tab provides agricultural credit scoring based on your soil quality, crop health, and insurance history. We partner with microfinance institutions to offer simplified credit processes. Maintain active insurance for 6 months to become eligible for financing opportunities.
